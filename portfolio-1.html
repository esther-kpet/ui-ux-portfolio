Project Overview

FamDinn is a family dinner ordering app that streamlines the process of ordering food for your family gathering. With FamDinn, you can easily browse menus and place orders from your favorite local restaurants and caterers, all in one convenient location.


Problem Statement:

Many families, students, and friends who want to bond or celebrate special occasions often find it challenging to plan and order food from multiple restaurants or caterers. This can result in a time-consuming and stressful process of coordinating orders, menus, and deliveries, which may also limit the food options available. FamDinn aims to address this problem by providing a user-friendly platform that enables families, students, and friends to easily order food from their favorite local restaurants and caterers, without the hassle of coordinating multiple orders or menus. The app offers a variety of food options to facilitate bonding and enhance family gatherings, enabling users to order with ease, whether they want to surprise their parents or enjoy a meal together without cooking at home.


Challenges:

  1. Restaurant partnerships: One of the biggest challenges will be to establish partnerships with local restaurants and caterers in the areas where the app is offered. FamDinn will need to convince restaurants and caterers to join the platform, which can be challenging given the existing competition in the market.
  2. Coordinating orders: Coordinating orders from multiple restaurants and caterers can be a complex process, particularly during peak times. FamDinn will need to develop an efficient order management system to ensure timely and accurate deliveries to customers.
  3. Payment and billing: FamDinn will need to develop a secure and user-friendly payment and billing system that can accommodate multiple transactions from different restaurants and caterers. This will require integrating various payment gateways and ensuring that transactions are transparent and accurate.
  4. User engagement: FamDinn will need to ensure that the app is user-friendly and engaging, particularly for younger users who may not be accustomed to ordering food online. This may require developing gamification features, offering discounts and rewards, and implementing a customer feedback system to improve user satisfaction.
  5. Scaling and expansion: As the app grows in popularity, FamDinn will need to scale up its infrastructure and operational capacity to handle increasing orders and users. This will require developing partnerships with more restaurants and caterers, expanding the delivery network, and ensuring that the platform can handle large volumes of transactions and data.
