Case Studies

Checkout My Case Studies

I dive deep into the projects that have been close to my heart. As a passionate UI/UX designer, I believe that case studies are not just a showcase of my skills, but a testament to the impact and value that well-executed design can bring to users and businesses alike. In each of these case studies, I have poured my heart into understanding the unique challenges faced by users and crafting solutions that go beyond aesthetics. I believe that good design is not just about making things look visually appealing, but about creating experiences that are intuitive, seamless, and meaningful.
Through these case studies, I aim to highlight the importance of user-centered design and how it can transform the way people interact with digital products.

  • FamDinn

    FamDinn is a family dinner ordering app that streamlines the process of ordering food for your family gathering. With FamDinn, you can easily browse menus and place orders from your favorite local restaurants and caterers, all in one convenient location.

  • Marv Movies

    Marv Movies is a free movie streaming platform. The platform aims to provide indie movie makers with a wide audience at the beginning of their careers. When users appreciate a certain movie/film, they can choose to follow the movie director, actors, scriptwriters, and everyone involved in their desired platform.

  • Mindful Pause

    The Mindful Pause app is designed to provide users with a quick and easy way to practice mindfulness and meditation during work breaks. With a variety of themes and durations to choose from, the app guides users through short exercises using audio guidance and visual cues to help them focus on the present moment.
